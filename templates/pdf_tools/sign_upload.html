What this tool does

Places a signature image overlay and optional date overlay on top of your PDF without rewriting original content blocks.

How to use

  1. Upload your PDF.
  2. Draw or upload a signature.
  3. Click one or more placement points and download.

Benefits

  • Supports multiple placements across pages.
  • Maintains document layout.
